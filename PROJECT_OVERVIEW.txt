UNIVERSAL BUSINESS BILLING & LEDGER SYSTEM - PROJECT OVERVIEW
========================================================================

1. PROJECT SUMMARY
------------------
A powerful, state-of-the-art billing and accountant ledger system designed 
for versatility across any business (Mobile, Jewelry, Retail, etc.). 
Built with a premium dark-mode interface and dynamic animations.

2. CORE MODULES
---------------
- DASHBOARD: Real-time tracking of Total Sales, Purchases, Net Profit, 
  and Total Cash. Includes a "Today's Status" summary and interactive 
  profit/loss charts.
- BILLING & INVOICES: A unified system for generating "Bills" and 
  "Tax Invoices". Features a 3-step smart checkout wizard (Form -> 
  Preview -> Share/Finalize).
- SALES LEDGER: A master history of all sales transactions with 
  advanced date-wise and type-wise filtering.
- PURCHASE LEDGER: Dedicated section to track stock entries and supplier 
  payments. Automatically calculates valuation based on purchase prices.
- CASH TRACKER: A digital cash book managing "Cash In", "Cash Out", 
  and "Opening Balances" with an automated running balance.
- INVENTORY (PRODUCTS & IMEI): Comprehensive stock management. Tracks 
  individual IMEI/Serial numbers with active/sold/inactive status tracking.
- CUSTOMERS & VENDORS: Robust contact management. Stores addresses, 
  GST numbers, and ID proofs. Includes smart validation for cross-module 
  email uniqueness.

3. KEY FEATURES & WORKING
-------------------------
- DOCUMENT GENERATION: 
  Professional PDF generation for Bills and Invoices. Includes business 
  branding (AMVIDIS INDIA logo), automated tax/discount calculations, 
  and professional table layouts.
- SMART AUTO-COMPLETE: 
  When entering a Sale, typing an IMEI automatically fetches the 
  Product Name and Purchase Price from the Inventory, preventing errors.
- METADATA ARCHITECTURE: 
  Uses a flexible JSONB storage system to track "Document Types" (Bill vs 
  Invoice) and contact details without needing complex database migrations.
- DYNAMIC PDF PREVIEW: 
  The "View" button in any ledger instantly regenerates a PDF preview 
  with options to Download, Print, or Share via WhatsApp and Email.
- BRAND INTEGRATION: 
  Custom logo placement and "Powered by Amvidis India" branding 
  integrated into the UI and PDF footers.
- ACCOUNTANT LEDGER LOGIC: 
  Automatically calculates Net Cash, Opening/Closing balances, and 
  Monthly Profit/Loss based on real-time data entry.

4. TECHNOLOGY STACK
-------------------
- Frontend: Next.js (React)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS (Modern Dark Mode / Glassmorphism)
- Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL / Real-time Data)
- PDF Engine: jsPDF & autoTable
- Icons/UI: Lucide React & Framer Motion

5. USER GUIDELINES
------------------
- To add a Sale: Click "+ SALE" and follow the wizard steps.
- To View/Resend: Hover over any transaction and click "👁️ View".
- Contact Uniqueness: An Email ID can be used once for a Customer 
  and once for a Vendor, but not twice within the same module.
- Search: Use the global search in any ledger to find records by 
  Name, IMEI, or Remark.
